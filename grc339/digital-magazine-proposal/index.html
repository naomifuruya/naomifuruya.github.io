Introduction

Japanese Internment Camps

On December 7th, 1941, Japan attacked the US, bombing Pearl Harbor and killing many innocent lives. This led to Order 9066 being established by President Franklin Roosevelt, causing thousands of Japanese Americans to be relocated from their homes to Internment Camps all throughout the United States. This website's intention is to bring awareness to what happened in 1942. The majority of history books don't even cover it, so hopefully you can learn something new from this.
