Introduction

All About Yosemite

This digital magazine displays all aspects of Yosemite – from trails to the best times to come. Yosemite National Park is California's most visited National Park, attracting individuals from all over the country. It can be overwhelming the first time you go because there are so many different trails and things to do, so the purpose of this magazine is to help individuals plan their trips with all of the information in one place and make it a lot easier.
